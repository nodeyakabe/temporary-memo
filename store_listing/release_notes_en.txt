Initial Release (v1.0.0)

Released "Temporary Memo" - a time-limited memo app!

【Key Features】
• Time-limited memos (1 hour ~ 7 days)
• Auto-delete when deadline is reached
• Privacy protection with biometric authentication
• Screenshot and screen recording prevention
• Home screen widget (up to 3 memos)
• Fully offline (no communication, no ads)

【Security Features】
• Biometric lock
• FLAG_SECURE (screen capture prevention)
• Backup disabled
• No internet permission

【Important Notice】
This app is intended for simple memo management and does not guarantee complete security. Data is not encrypted. For sensitive information, please use a dedicated password manager.

Thank you for using our app!
